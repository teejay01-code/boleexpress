About Us

At Bole Express, we bring the rich, smoky flavors of West Africa straight to your table with freshly roasted plantain and a variety of mouth-watery sides. We are not just a takeout spot, but we also offer delivery services for your convenience!

Planning a party or special event? We would love to be a part of your celebration! Whether it's a birthday, wedding, or coporate event, Bole Express provides party catering that will make your event unforgettable.

Founded by Mr & Mrs Jimoh Oluwasegun, our mission is to serve authentic bole, grilled food, and more to our community. With every bite, we aim to nourish both the body and soul through traditional dishes.
